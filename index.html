Client Case Study · USA · Confidential

From money-losing account
to 727 quality calls.

The Problem

A USA-based multi-city service business was spending heavily on Google Ads with broken conversion tracking. The algorithm optimized toward page views — not real leads. Real cost-per-customer exceeded $670 while Google falsely reported $103. Every dollar was burning with zero accountability.

What We Did

We rebuilt conversion tracking end-to-end via GTM so the algorithm could finally see real calls, form fills, and bookings. Eliminated junk spend with aggressive negative lists. Restructured into tightly themed, location-specific campaigns across two states and let the algorithm re-learn on clean data.

The Result

Over 7 months: 727 quality calls (genuine inquiries over 10 seconds). Cost-per-quality-call fell 25%. For the first time, every dollar of ad spend was tied to a measurable business outcome.
